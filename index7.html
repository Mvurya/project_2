FAQs

1. What is the best way to clean my spectacles? To clean your spectacles effectively, follow these steps: Rinse your glasses with lukewarm water to remove any debris or dirt. Apply a small drop of mild dish soap to each lens. Gently lather the soap on both sides of the lenses and frames with your fingers. Rinse the glasses thoroughly with lukewarm water. Dry your spectacles with a clean microfiber cloth to prevent streaks.

2. How do I know if my spectacles fit properly? Properly fitting spectacles should: Rest comfortably on the bridge of your nose without pinching. Sit securely on your ears without sliding down. Allow you to see clearly through the center of the lenses without any distortion. Have temples that rest lightly against your head without causing pressure points.

3. Can I replace the lenses in my spectacles? Yes, most spectacles allow for lens replacement. You can consult with our customer service team or visit one of our stores for assistance in selecting and replacing lenses for your frames.

4. How should I store my spectacles when not in use? To keep your spectacles safe when not in use: Store them in a hard-shell case to prevent scratches or damage. Avoid placing them face down on surfaces to prevent lens scratches. Keep them away from extreme heat or direct sunlight to avoid warping or discoloration.

5. Do you offer prescription lenses for spectacles? Yes, we offer prescription lenses for our spectacles. You can provide us with your prescription details, and we will assist you in selecting the appropriate lenses for your needs.
